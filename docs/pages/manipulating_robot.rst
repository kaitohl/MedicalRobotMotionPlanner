Manipulating The Robot
=======================

After selecting your robot, you get access to the "Joint Control" and "3D Control" tabs. The "Joint Control" tab allows you to control the robot by directly manipulating the joint values, while the "3D Control" tab allows you to control the robot by manipulating the end effector in 3D space.

Joint Control
----------------

Once a robot is selected, the "Joint Control" tab will populate with sliders for each joint of the robot. You can use these sliders to manipulate the joint values of the robot, and see the changes in real time in the 3D view. The joint values are shown in degrees, and the sliders are automatically scaled to the joint limits of the robot. You can also input specific joint values in the text boxes next to the sliders. There is an additional "Home" button that will reset all the joint values to their zero position.
